Target list

To add new targets you can either got to the recent attacks page and save them with the button at the end of each row or add the ID at the end of the target list. Click on the row to refresh the information.
The results correspond to the latest attack made. If no attack has been found the respect is computed from the target level (base respect).
If the timer starts to blink it means that the target might be out of the hospital.
For the attack list, the level is guessed from the base respect so because of rounding errors it might be off ± 1.

The Flat respect is computed here without any multipliers except the fair fight \(m_\text{fair}\). It yields: $$R_\text{flat} = m_\text{fair} R_\text{b} = m_\text{fair}\times(\ln(l)+1)/4$$ In case no recent attack has been found, we take \(m_\text{fair}=1\).

Target list

Here you find a list of your saved targets.

Description of headings

  • Last Attack - When you last performed a successful attack against the player
  • Result - Attacked, Mugged, Hospitalized
  • Name - Hyperlink to their torn profile
  • Level - Current level
  • Last Action - The time since their last action
  • Fair Fight - The fair fight bonus you received on your last successful attack (or 1 if no successful attack recorded)
  • Flat Respect - Their base level respect multiplied by the Fair Fight bonus
  • Life - Current life
  • Status - Their current status and remaining time (if hospitalized)
  • Update - Time since the last update (refresh) was performed
  • Notes - Allows you to enter custom notes for the target
  • Attack - Direct link to their attack loader
  • Delete - Link to remove them from your target list

Building your list

There are two ways to add targets:

  1. Enter a Torn ID and click "Add" at the bottom of the list
  2. Click "Attacks" to bring up your list of recent attcks. Find a target and click on the "save" slider. You can sort this attacks list by clicking on any of the headings.

How to use this list

Once you have your list built, it will show all targets either in black or gray text. Black text means the target status has been recently updated. After 2 hours, the text will be gray meaning you should refresh the target to get an accurate status.

To refresh the status of the targets, you can either:

  • Click anywhere on the target except a hyperlink/icon to refresh a single target
  • Click "Refresh All" to refresh all of your targets

From your list, you can either click on the user's name to bring up their profile or you can go directly to their attack loader by clicking the crosshairs. If the crosshairs () are red, they are likely ready to be attacked. If the crossharis are blue, they are likely unable to be attacked. If the target’s status and update is flashing red, they are likely out of the hospital based on the time elapsed since the last update.

Daystar's tips for chaining

  • Sort your list by flat respect
  • Add notes that are useful. For example – mitigated, high strength, etc.
  • Keep at least 40 targets in your list. That way, you can start with a full stack and run through your list. By the time you are ready for a refill/Xanax, your first targets should be coming out of the hospital (assuming you left them)
  • Keep some weaker targets for when you are on Xanax Cooldown or need to save your Med Cooldown

FAQ

  • The "Last attack", "Result", "Fair fight" and "Flat Respect" of my target is not updating: make sure you visit the Attacks page before. That's when target's information related to you are updated.
  • The "Level" is wrong on the Attacks page: it is calculated based on the base respect, so it happens because of rounding errors. It will be the good one on the Targets page
