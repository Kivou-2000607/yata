 Tutorial

Data is taken from armory news and funds news.

Transactions handled

  • A member deposited money, points or an item.
  • A member used an item or points (refill).
  • A member filled a blood bag.
  • A member was given money.

Transactions not handled

  • A member loaned an item.
  • A member returned an item.
  • A member retrieved an item from another member.
  • A member gave an item to another member.
  • A member opened a cache.
  • A member adjusted the balance of another member.
