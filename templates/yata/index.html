YATA stands for Yet Another Torn App. It has been designed to enhance your gameplay using information from the official Torn API.
To see what's in there browse through the following categories.
Some parts are public and you can browse them anonymously but if you want to unleash the full power of YATA you need to enter your API key.

 Home private
{# home #}
Checkout your personal stats and use the simulators.
{# bazaar #}
 Bazaar limited
Look at the current bazaar prices and market value tendencies to sell or buy at the best prices.
{# company #}
 Company limited
Supervise your company and compare your work stats to job positions.
{# faction #}
 Faction private
Build your chain, attacks and revives reports, monitor your members and armory, share your spies and manage your perks.
{# targets #}
 Targets private
Build up a target list and maximize your respect gains during chains, manage your revives and losses.
{# awards #}
 Awards limited
Track your honors and medals and check which one you can get next.
{# loot #}
 Loot public
Checkout the timing of NPC's loot levels.
