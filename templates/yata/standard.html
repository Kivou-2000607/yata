YATA stands for Yet Another Torn App. It has been designed to enhance your gameplay using information from the official Torn API.
To see what's in there browse through the following categories.
Some parts are public and you can browse them anonymously but if you want to unleash the full power of YATA you need to enter your API key.

Homeprivate
Checkout your personal stats and use the simulators.

Bazaar overviewlimited
Look at the current bazaar prices and market value tendencies to sell or buy at the best prices.

Stock overviewlimited
View all the stock shares, prices and tendencies.

Company overviewlimited
Supervise your company (wip).

Factionprivate
Build your chain reports, combine them and monitor your faction.

Targetsprivate
Remember your good targets and maximize your respect gains during chains.

Awardslimited
Track the next award you can get.

Lootpublic
Gives the timing of when NPCs are reaching the loot levels.

Discord botlimited
Discord bot made for Torn.

APIpublic
Interacts with the YATA database for loot, foreign stocks and more.
