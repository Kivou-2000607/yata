The YATA bot is a discord bot made for Torn.
It has a set of appplication commands made to facilitate your Torn life while keeping your server secure.
It has been designed to fully use the new interactions features of discord, minimizing the amount of tying and offering a nice UX, even on mobile.
For any questions that the documentation failed to answer, for suggestions or bug reports please join the Yata discord server.
