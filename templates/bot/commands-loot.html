Module Loot  

This module gives timings of NPC loot levels and notify.

Command !loot  

Gives a report for all NPC with timings to loot level IV.

Syntax: !loot

Aliases: !duke, !Duke, !leslie, !Leslie, !Loot

NPC report of 19/12/05 21:01:03 TCT for Kivou [2000607]
Duke   : [====================] (100%) 00hrs 02mins 03s since loot level IV [20:59:00 TCT]
Scrooge: [=================   ] ( 88%) 00hrs 24mins 26s to loot level IV [21:25:29 TCT]
Leslie : [                    ] (  0%) 04hrs 27mins 49s to loot level IV [01:28:52 TCT]

Command !assign loot  

Gives or removes the looter role of the command author.

Syntax: !assign loot

Notifications  

Notifies the looter role when an NPC is 5 minutes before loot level IV.
