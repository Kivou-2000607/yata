Module Stocks  

This module gives information on wssb and tcb sharing group and notifies good investments on the stock market.

Command !wssb  

Give education's time left and BB ownership for all that have the wssb role.

Syntax: !wssb

NAME            | EDU TIME LEFT | WSSB
 --------------------------------------
userA           |  0d  5h 31min |  x
userB           |  5d 15h 56min |
userC           |  8d  8h 15min |
userD           |  8d 11h 55min |  x

Command !tcb  

Give bank investment's time left and BB ownership for all that have the tcb role.

Syntax: !tcb

NAME            | INV TIME LEFT | TCB
--------------------------------------
userA           | 10d 13h 13min |
userB           | 14d 15h 54min |  x
userC           | 20d 16h 56min |

Command !assign stocks  

Gives or removes the stocks role of the command author.

Syntax: !assign stocks

Notifications  

Notifies the stocks role for good investments on the stock market. Triggers are when:

  • forecast from bad to avg / good & shares available & price lower than trimestrial average
  • new shares are injected by the system
