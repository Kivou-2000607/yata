 Host the YATA bot

You can host the YATA bot on your server.
It's free and it's easy, just follow these steps:

  1. Read the documentation.
  2. Invite the bot to your server.
  3. Log into YATA and check out below that your discord account is verified by Torn.
  4. Join the YATA server and open an ticket for a bot setup.
    Give us the name of your server and no PM please.
  5. Visit the dashboard to configure your bot.
    Do an initial !sync on your server first.
  6. Read the documentation.
  7. Enjoy!
