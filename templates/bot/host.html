Host the YATA bot

{% include "svg/yata.bot.logo.svg" %}You can host the YATA bot on your server.
It's free and it's easy, just follow these steps:

  1. Read the documentation.
  2. Be verified on the official Torn discord.
    You can check your status just below.
  3. Log into YATA.
  4. Invite the bot to your server.
  5. Join the YATA server and kindly ask for an @Helper to set you up as an admin.
    Give us the name of your server.
  6. Visit the dashboard to configure your bot.
    Do an initial !sync first.
  7. Read the documentation.
  8. Enjoy!
