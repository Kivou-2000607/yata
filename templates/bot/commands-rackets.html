 Module Rackets  

Command !assign rackets  

Gives or removes the rackets role of the command author.

Syntax: !assign rackets

Notifications  

Notifies the racket role when an event happens on a territory with a racket. Events are:

  • A racket spawns
  • A racket vanishes
  • A racket moves up one level
  • A racket moves down one level
  • A war starts for a racket
