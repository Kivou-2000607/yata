Awards Tracker

All awards data are updated upon refresh of the page.
If you put your cursor hover the last column, you'll have an explanation.
You can show the acheived awards by clicking on the top right section title.
The Hall of Fame rarity score \(R\) is computed as follow: $$R = {\sum_{a\in\mathcal{P}}r(a)} \ \Big/ \ {\sum_{a\in\mathcal{A}}r(a)}$$ with \(n(a)\), the number of player having the award \(a\), \(r(a)=1/\ln(n(a)+1)\) the rarity score of award \(a\), \(\mathcal{A}\) the set of all reachable awards, \(\mathcal{P}\subset\mathcal{A}\) the set of all the awards you have.
If you put your cursor hover the column rarity you'll have the contribution of each honor to this score.
HoF is updated daily and considers only players who went to the Awards Tracker page.
